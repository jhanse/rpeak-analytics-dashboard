    Metric notes. Setup target days = the later of HRA approval date and Date site selected to email confirmation of capacity and capability where available, otherwise a transparent fallback date; incomplete endpoint tasks are counted to today, not to due date. Task-level durations remain task-specific, using each task’s own start/completion dates rather than resetting every task to HRA approval. Green→1st patient = green-light completion date to first-patient completion date where present; incomplete endpoint tasks are counted to today, not to due date. This is used as the 30-day recruitment target. Bottleneck score is a prioritisation signal combining active backlog, overdue/over-target delay, records active >60 days, delayed-task volume, and contribution to projects missing 60/30 targets. Resource score is the same concept aggregated by task theme/category. These are triage metrics, not formal performance validation.
